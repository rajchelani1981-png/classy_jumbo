Who We Are

JUMBOMASTER Group of Companies Pvt Ltd is a professional and internationally active manufacturer, exporter, and distributor of food products. The company was established in 2014

We specialize in confectionery, canned food, and processed food products. With a diverse portfolio of nearly 200+ products, we cater to a wide range of consumer needs across global markets.

Our product assortment includes biscuits, cookies, chocolates, candies, lollipops, bubble gum, peanut butter, honey, instant drink powders, pineapple, sweet corn, olive oil, juices, and more.
